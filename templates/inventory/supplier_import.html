Sample CSV Format

Your CSV file should include the following columns:

supplier_code name supplier_type contact_person email phone address_line1 city state postal_code payment_terms
SUP001 Auto Parts Plus distributor John Smith john@autoparts.com (555) 123-4567 123 Main St Anytown CA 12345 Net 30
SUP002 Motor Supply Co wholesaler Jane Doe jane@motorsupply.com (555) 987-6543 456 Oak Ave Another City TX 67890 COD
Column Descriptions:
  • supplier_code: Unique identifier (required)
  • name: Supplier company name (required)
  • supplier_type: manufacturer, distributor, wholesaler, retailer, or other
  • contact_person: Primary contact name
  • email: Contact email address
  • phone: Contact phone number
  • address_line1: Street address
  • city: City name
  • state: State or province
  • postal_code: ZIP or postal code
  • payment_terms: Payment terms (e.g., Net 30, COD)
